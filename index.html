A Little Bit About Bella

When combining a quiet creative, a first gen college grad in the STEM field, and years in the tech corporate world; Bella had developed quite the diverse background to say the least. Always aiming for one foot in front of the other, but a lingering theme that reoccured in her mind was: “Where do I fit in?”. A valid question! Bella consistently brainstormed, “where can I can combine my research and analytical skills, with my creative talents? Where can I make the most impact with my strengths?” If there was one aspect of 2020 to be thankful for, it would be the clarity to discover a passion for UX UI Design. Fast forward to today, Bella is soon to become a graduate of the University of Arizona’s UX Bootcamp this February 2021. As luck would have it, her diverse background gave her the foundational skills that helped her find a passion for problem solving, data driven solutions, and user focused design. UX Design, the perfect match.
